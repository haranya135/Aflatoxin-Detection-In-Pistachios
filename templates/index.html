The Problem

Food Adulteration is a Silent Threat

Food adulteration — the intentional addition of inferior, harmful, or foreign substances — affects millions globally. From synthetic dyes in spices to chemical preservatives in dairy, these invisible contaminants pose serious health risks that traditional inspection methods fail to catch.

🌶️
Spice & Condiment Adulteration
Sudan dyes, chalk powder, and artificial colorants mixed into spices to enhance appearance and cut costs.
🥛
Dairy Product Contamination
Melamine, starch, and detergents added to milk and dairy products to inflate protein content readings.
🫒
Oil & Fat Substitution
Cheaper oils blended into premium varieties like olive oil, argan oil, and ghee without disclosure.
🍯
Sweetener Manipulation
High-fructose corn syrup and rice syrup used to dilute natural honey, undermining its health value.
